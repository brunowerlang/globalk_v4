Open Box Operation

Our Open Box Operation focuses on giving products a second chance. We acquire returned items with open boxes from major marketplaces like Via Varejo, Casas Bahia, Mercado Livre, and Magazine Luiza. Each product undergoes a thorough inspection to ensure quality and check for any missing parts. After repackaging, these items are made available at competitive prices through our platform, Economize.

This approach supports sustainable shopping and provides consumers with quality products at lower costs. Small outlets also benefit by having access to these exclusive items for resale, maximizing value for everyone involved.
