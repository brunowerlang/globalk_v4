2013: HP & GLOBALK Agreement

HP and GLOBALK signed a Compaq Brand licensing agreement in September 2013. They began developing the Compaq product line in Brazil with a focus on notebooks, detachables, desktops, and tablets.

In partnership with HP Brazil, they developed local products under the HP Brand, including notable projects such as the Ipanema, Copacabana, and Leblon projects.

2016: Outlet in Sorocaba

In August 2016, they opened an official outlet in Sorocaba. The outlet focused on refurbished HP products and offered quality products at affordable prices.

Website: www.sincplace.com.br

2017: E-Commerce and Mercado Livre

In September 2017, they expanded into e-commerce and began operations on Mercado Livre, Brazil's leading online marketplace, offering a wide range of refurbished HP products.

Open Box Operation

At the core of our business, we focus on direct sales strategies tailored for both B2B and B2C markets. Our model supports small outlets through B2B relationships, ensuring they have access to high-quality products at competitive prices. We also focus on B2C strategies, encouraging customers to bypass marketplaces and shop directly from trusted sources.

Our B2B approach enables small outlets to thrive by offering exclusive products, better margins, and efficient delivery solutions. Meanwhile, for consumers, we emphasize the value of avoiding large online marketplaces and making purchases directly through our platform, where savings and customer service are our top priorities.
